Conditioning the process to hit a half-space


You are almost surely visiting this cause you scanned a QR code on my poster about Excursions from hyperplanes. Thanks for your interest! Here's a digital copy of it.

One of the main applications of developing a theory of excursions from directional extrema is to condition the α-stable process to do some really weird behaviour. But, hey, that's what distinguishes probabilists from measure-theorists. Such conditioning is to hit a given subset of a hyperplane and then die. See below a gif illustrating the idea.

If you are still interested in this application and its mathematical formulation take a look into the Section 3.2 of my confirmation report.
